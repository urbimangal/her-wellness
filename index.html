Is it normal to feel more tired during my fertile window?
Yes, that's common. Hormone shifts around ovulation — particularly a rise in progesterone afterward — can make you feel more tired. Staying hydrated and prioritizing sleep usually helps. This isn't medical advice — check with your doctor if fatigue feels severe.
